About Me

I’m Michael Adeyeye

A passionate product and graphics designer whose goal and aim is to create solutions to the pain points of every users out there and to create a visual communicating solutions to startups and scaleups brands and organizations in order to improve their customer experience across channels.

I am a self-thought design genius with the best design toolkit i.e inspiration from God as I believe ideating and imaginations comes from him.

I’m driven by creating new innovative products and services that put user experience at the center.

I was born and raised in Nigeria. After personal struggles and frustration, I ventured into the world of design. I believe everyone has a story to tell. My journey into design world began when all hope seems to be lost but God showed up.

My love for design kept increasing when I see the way people think and respond to products and how designers could help them live a more fulfilling lives. I have learn to put user front and center.

I have had the opportunity to work for startups brands and companies both local and international. I am responsible for the visual foundational identity of many brands out there.

Outside of design, I am a Christian and a lover of God. I am a Discipler whose assignment is to raise men and women who will be fit into God’s purposes for their lives. I am a husband of one beautiful wife and a father to a handsome boy.

I studied Business Administration in Moshood Abiola Polytechnic, Abeokuta, Ogun State.
I believe in the saying that no one is an isle of knowledge and for that reason I am always ready to learn and impact others as well.
