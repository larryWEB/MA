TSCH

TSCH is a non-government organization whose aim and goal is to help the less privilege and in giving them hope. Their mission is to give Education, Water and Health services to those who could not afford it.

The Overview

We have a lot of people with a good heart willing to do good deeds. We want the change to start with us. In today’s world, we have a lot of people who have been lifted up by those with the heart of love.

With the introduction of internet and NGO. users are increasingly showing their heart of kindness by making their donation online. Though there are some challenges donor faces some of which are:

  • Cannot donate anonymously.
  • Cannot track donations.
  • Unsecure donations.
  • Distance barrier .

Solution

Design a responsive donation website that allows donors to donate anonymously and also in memory, donate items, considering transparency to protect user data.

My Role

Conducting interviews, paper and digital wire-frame, low and high-fidelity prototyping, conducting usability studies, accounting for accessibility, and iterating on designs.

Design Process

It is time to start my design, bring solution but as a designer there’re steps to take in order to solve and meet the needs of the users.

Duration

Six Weeks

Qualitative Research

  • I want to understand factors that hinders donors from making donations.
  • To make research on what to do in order to help donors make donation at their comfort.

    • Interview Questions

    In the research phase, I interviewed four people of different age in order to understand their challenges, pain points and goals. In this phase I made sure to watch after myself so that I won’t be bias. I also got the consent of my respondents I interview to record their voice and share with the team. Some of the interview questions.

    What are your concerns for the less privilege?

    What factors do you consider on a NGO website?

    Why wont you use an online donation website?

    How often do you donate or give?

    What will make you donate more online?

    Would you prefer to make donation online or visit NGO?

    Target Audience

      User Pain points

    • I would like to donate but most NGO don't allow donation in memory or honor.
    • Don’t have time visiting NGO due to my tight schedules.
    • Insecure payment options.

    User Research Summary

    I started my research by checking and analyzing website structure of few NGO’S websites and see where there is need for improvement. By doing this I was able to come up with a better design to give users a good experience when browsing on the websites.

    Empathy Map

    The empathy map help me to understand the reasons behind donor’s action and it helps me to focus on what to design.

    Site-Map

    This is the steps and decisions a user makes when they visit the websites. It will orientates the users and help improve site navigation. For this project, I used the Hierarchical Websites Structure. (Body)
